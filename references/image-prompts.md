# Illustration prompt

Generate each scene as a square 1:1 illustration-only plate.

Use this invariant style block:

> Colorful children’s storybook illustration drawn with graphite pencil outlines and visible
> colored-pencil/crayon texture. Warm, playful, expressive animal characters, simple readable
> silhouettes, gentle imperfect hand-made strokes, sparse forest details, clean pale cream-white
> background. Full bodies and important objects completely inside the frame with generous margins.
> No typography, no letters, no captions, no border, no panel layout, no watermark, no photorealism,
> no 3D render.

Append the scene-specific `visual` description. Keep recurring characters visually consistent:

- fox: orange-red fur, white chest and tail tip, clever expressive face;
- tiger: warm orange coat, black stripes, white muzzle and belly, large but child-friendly;
- preserve clothing, proportions, and colors across scenes if the story introduces them.

Use the first approved image as a visual reference for later scenes when the image tool supports
references. Reject images containing text, cropped characters, dark/complex backgrounds, or
photorealistic rendering.
